The Miles Hobby Blog

My Name is Jack Miles and welcome to my Hobby Blog. I have been playing Tabletop wargames and painting models since I was around 12 years old.
I started out playing the WWII game "Flames of War" developed by New Zealand Company Battlefront Miniatures Ltd.
Shortly after starting I fell in love with Warhammer 40k and since then I have played a few different armies, namely Imperial Guard, Eldar, Adeptus Mechanicus and now Space Marines.

Previous Next

I'm constanly working to improve my painting techniques. Each of my armies I paint I choose a technique to develop for with that army.
I chose a white colour scheme for my Adeptus Mechanicus as white is a difficult colour to paint, it requires many thin layers and much patience!
For my Space Marines I wanted to learn how to paint yellow, which is similar in difficulty to white. For this reason I chose to paint my Marines as the Scythes of the Emperor Chapter.
The Scythes of the Emperor also have reallly cool lore dating all the way back to the Horus Heresy. They are featured in the HH book Pharos where they attempt to fight off a Night Lords invasion force hell-bent on claiming the Pharos device. I'm unashamedly an Ultramarines fan(Fight me) and the idea of a first founding successor chapter related to the Space Roman Bois, along with great lore and a cool paint scheme solidified my decision to start the Scythes of the Emperor Chapter.
