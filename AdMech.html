Adeptus Mechanicus

Here I present to you my forces of the Adeptus Mechanicus called the Scions of Cawl. The Scions of Cawl are a splinter faction of the adeptus mechanicus. They to not only persue knowledtge like the rest of the Adeptus Mechanicus, but to also innovate and improve on current technologies of the Imperium. This viewed as heretical by the rest of the Adeptus Mechanicus, who believe that all of mankinds technology is currently perfect in the eyes of the Ommnissiah. The Scions of Cawl postulate however, that the greatest creation of the Ommnissiah is the Human brain and this overrides all other creations of the Ommnissiah. due to their standing with the rest of their faction, the Scions of Cawl have made their home in an Asteroid, named Omega Questus that they have outfitted to undertake both space and warp travel. They wear white as a their main colour as they have recovered ancient artifacts and knowledge that show tech-priest form Terra's distant past wearing white coats. As the Tech-priest revere this age of knowledge and enlightenment, they bedeck themselves in similar white coats.
